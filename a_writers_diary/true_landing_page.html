How to create a landing page
Hi friend, I'm here to show you a really simple landing page, so stay with me!

You can persuade your prospect to take an action!

First thing first, BE NICE TO READER MIND

Do NOT use long sentences. If you choose a long sentences your prospect mind becomes tired. They don't remember more than 20 words per sentences. So keep it SHORT. You want to present/sell something, Not writing scientific articles.

Be nice to reader EYE

A paragraph can be a single sentences!

As long as there are white spaces the reading is much better. Remember You are NOT writing scientific articles!

Use white spaces wisely. Spaces: The words spaces, the paragraph and sentence spaces and graphical white spaces!

It's all about them, NOT you!

That's obvious, your product/service exist to serves your customer.

You write about them by writing about the problem you solve.

Use graphics WISELY

You can add as much as graphics NEEDED, no more or less.

Graphics should serves two things:

  • FLOW: graphics should follow the page's purpose (selling like product's picture, signup, join, etc).
  • DESIGN: graphics should follow the design you chose (make page prettier, readability, etc).

Whatever graphic you want to put in the page, should follows the two rules above.

REMEMBER YOU ARE PRESENTING ON A PURPOSE, NOT FOR FUN.

Be nice and friend with your reader/customer

Again you are writing for people for customer, you are not writing a scientific article.

Think him/her as a friend, a dear friend. How would you speak to your friend?

Also be nice and polite, like a very dear friend.

Write as you speak, not as you write! This means writing like everyday talk, like presenting, not like writing a book.
