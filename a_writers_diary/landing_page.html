A simple normal landing page

Although it's not a TRUE landing page

Normally a landing page exist to promote

To promote something, for example to sell product or signup to news letter, or even joining an event!

The page should have a call to action (CTA) button, also it need some COPYWRITING on page to convince people to do an action (buy, join, signup, etc).

A TRUE LANDING PAGE has no other link than the CTAs. The nature of an landing page is to persuade people to do something, so any other link will be an distraction. The prospect should only see one link (maybe many CTA pointing to only one link or action).
